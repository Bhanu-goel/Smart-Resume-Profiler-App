Position: Software Engineer

Job Summary: We are seeking a talented Software Engineer to join our development team. The ideal candidate will have a strong foundation in software engineering principles, as well as experience in full-stack development. The candidate will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining software applications and ensuring the quality and efficiency of code.

Key Responsibilities:

Design, develop, and maintain software applications.
Collaborate with product teams to translate business requirements into technical solutions.
Write clean, scalable, and efficient code.
Work with both front-end and back-end technologies to develop full-stack applications.
Utilize web frameworks such as Django, Flask, or React for development.
Develop and maintain databases using MySQL, PostgreSQL, or MongoDB.
Utilize cloud platforms like AWS or Azure for deployment and scaling.
Perform code reviews and ensure best practices are followed in the software development process.
Required Skills & Qualifications:

Strong programming skills in languages like Python, Java, or C++.
Proficiency in web frameworks such as Django, Flask, or React.
Experience with relational and non-relational databases such as MySQL, PostgreSQL, or MongoDB.
Familiarity with version control tools, especially Git.
Knowledge of cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud is a plus.
Strong problem-solving skills and ability to debug and optimize code.
Excellent teamwork and collaboration skills.
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.
